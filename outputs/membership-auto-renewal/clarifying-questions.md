# Clarifying Questions

## Must Answer Before Launch

1. What is the current renewal success rate and target improvement window?
2. Which payment failure reasons are most common: expired card, insufficient funds, bank decline, or processor error?
3. What renewal reminder channels are approved: in-app, H5, email, SMS, push, or all?
4. What legal copy is required for renewal price, billing date, cancellation, and refund policy?
5. Which regions, payment methods, and subscription plans are in scope?

## Can Proceed With Assumptions for Draft

1. H5 is the primary prototype platform.
2. The first release focuses on members with upcoming renewal or failed renewal.
3. The solution may include reminder copy, payment update, benefit recap, and confirmation states.
4. Existing cancellation and refund entry points remain available.
5. No new pricing model is introduced.

## Can Decide Later

1. Exact notification copy variants.
2. Experiment split ratio.
3. Whether to include coupons or retention offers.
4. Whether to add native App implementation after H5 validation.
