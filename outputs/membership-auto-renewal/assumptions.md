# Assumptions

| ID | Assumption | Reason | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | H5 is the first platform. | The task brief states mobile web entry is common. | Native App behavior may need a separate pass. |
| A2 | The main problem is preventable renewal failure, not lack of product value. | The request mentions auto-renewal conversion. | If churn is value-driven, payment optimization will not solve it. |
| A3 | Renewal price and billing cycle are already known to the system. | Subscription products typically store plan details. | Missing billing data blocks transparent confirmation copy. |
| A4 | Cancellation access must remain visible. | Subscription flows can create trust and compliance risk. | Legal review is required before launch. |
| A5 | No raw payment data will be collected in analytics. | Guardrails forbid sensitive payment fields. | Analytics detail may be limited for payment diagnostics. |
