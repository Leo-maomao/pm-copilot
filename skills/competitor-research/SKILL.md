# Competitor Research

## Goal

Provide useful external context without fabricating sources or claims.

## Workflow

1. Define the research question.
2. Identify known competitors from context and likely comparison categories.
3. Use available search or browsing tools.
4. Capture source title, URL, date if available, observed fact, and confidence.
5. Convert findings into product implications.

## Output

- Research question
- Source list
- Competitor comparison table
- Product implications
- Limitations and unknowns

## Quality Bar

- Source-backed claims include references.
- Unavailable information is marked unavailable.
- Implications are tied to the current product task.
