# Privacy Policy for Local Use

PM Copilot is local-first in v1. The repository does not include cloud storage, authentication, or telemetry.

## Recommended User Behavior

- Use synthetic, anonymized, or sampled data in public examples.
- Remove real customer names, emails, phone numbers, payment details, and partner secrets.
- Do not commit private product strategy or unreleased financial details to a public fork.

## Agent Behavior

- Warn before handling sensitive data.
- Prefer aggregated metrics over raw user-level data.
- Use placeholder values in examples.
- Include privacy notes in tracking plans.
