# Task Brief: Membership Auto-Renewal Optimization

## Raw Request

We want to optimize membership auto-renewal because renewal conversion looks lower than expected. Please create a PRD and related review materials.

## Product Area

Subscription membership.

## Target Platform

H5 first. The flow is commonly entered from mobile web push, in-app message, SMS, or email links.

## Known Goal

Improve successful membership renewal without increasing complaints, refund requests, or accidental renewal perception.

## Known Users

- Current paying members with auto-renewal enabled.
- Current paying members whose payment method is close to expiry or recently failed.
- Recently expired members who can still renew.

## Constraints

- The flow must not hide cancellation or billing information.
- Payment, cancellation, and consent copy require human review.
- Tracking must avoid raw payment details and unnecessary personal data.

## Requested Artifacts

- Clarifying questions
- Assumptions
- PRD
- Metrics tree
- Tracking plan
- Mermaid user flow
- H5 low-fidelity HTML prototype
- Review checklist
- Final package summary
